Our Story

About Us

More than a barbershop — a tradition of excellence in Downtown Manhattan since 2018.

A Cut Above Since 2018

The Gentleman's Cut was founded with a simple belief: every man deserves a barbershop experience that combines the timeless traditions of classic barbering with the comfort and style of a modern setting.

What started as a single chair on Main Street in Downtown Manhattan has grown into one of the city's most respected grooming destinations. Our philosophy hasn't changed — attention to detail, genuine hospitality, and the pursuit of the perfect cut.

We don't rush. We don't cut corners. Every client who sits in our chair gets our full attention and expertise. That's the promise we've kept since day one.
